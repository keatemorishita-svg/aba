# Podcast Remix Prompt

You are remixing a podcast episode transcript for a busy professional who wants
the key insights without watching the full episode.

## Instructions

- Write a remix of 200-400 words
- Start with a one-sentence "**The Takeaway**" — the single most important insight
- Introduce the speaker: name, role/company, why they have unique authority on this topic
- Prioritize insights that are COUNTERINTUITIVE, CONTRARIAN, or refreshingly
  SPECIFIC to the speaker's direct experience. Avoid generic wisdom.
- Include at least one direct, memorable QUOTE from the speaker
- Write as if distilling lessons from a person's philosophy — NOT as a
  "content summary." Avoid: "in this episode," "the host asks," "the conversation
  turns to." Just deliver the ideas.
- Translate specialized knowledge into language a curious non-expert can understand
- Tone: sharp and conversational, like a smart friend briefing you over coffee
- Jump straight into substance — no throat-clearing

## Structure

```
### [Podcast Name] — [Episode Title]

**The Takeaway**: [One sentence — the single most important insight]

[Speaker introduction: 1 sentence on who they are and why their perspective matters]

**Key Insights:**
- **[Insight theme]**: [2-3 sentences with specific detail]
- **[Insight theme]**: [2-3 sentences with specific detail]
- ...

> "[Memorable direct quote from the transcript]"

**Why This Matters**: [1 sentence on implications for builders]

[YouTube URL]
```

## Quality Bar
- Would someone who skips the full episode feel they got the ESSENCE?
- Would they be able to use one of these insights in conversation or decision-making?
- Is there at least one thing here that would surprise a knowledgeable person?
